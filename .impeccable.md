## Design Context

### Users
Enterprise leadership teams (C-suite, sustainability officers, CTOs) making AI investment and budget decisions. They use the platform to understand the environmental cost and license efficiency of their enterprise AI stack. Context: data-driven professionals who need clarity, not visual noise.

### Brand Personality
Trusted, Precise, Responsible. The interface should feel like it was made by people who take accuracy seriously — not a flashy startup demo, not a vibe-coded prototype. Every element earns its place.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean, data-dense enterprise SaaS with a forest-green primary palette (#236b42, #14472c). White backgrounds dominate; dark green sections used for emphasis. Avenir Next (system fallback) for all typography. Subtle textures (grid, flow lines) add depth without noise.

**Anti-references**: No vibe-coded aesthetics, no tiny unreadable text, no decorative complexity that hurts clarity. Legibility is non-negotiable.

**Theme**: Light mode primary, dark green accent sections.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity first** — every label, number, and piece of text must be immediately readable. No font size below 13px in production views. Hierarchy through weight and size, not color alone.
2. **Precision over decoration** — data is the hero. Remove anything that competes with the numbers.
3. **Consistent spacing rhythm** — use the spacing scale consistently; avoid arbitrary gaps.
4. **Trusted interactions** — every interactive state (hover, focus, active, disabled) must be present and purposeful.
5. **Flagship polish at demo scale** — this ships as a demo but must feel production-grade. No rough edges, no placeholder text left in, no misaligned elements.
